Privacy Policy for Quit Smoking 30

Last updated: December 2025

Quit Smoking 30 (“the App”) helps users gradually reduce smoking using a break-based motivational plan. The App does not provide medical advice. For help with nicotine addiction, please consult a healthcare professional.

Data We Collect

The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a unique anonymous identifier (UID). No personal data such as name, email, phone number, or address is collected.

We store the following non-personal usage data:

  • Number of cigarettes smoked and allowed per day
  • App settings and notification preferences
  • Progress tracking and plan structure

This data is stored securely in Firebase and used only to provide the App’s features.

How We Use the Data

  • To create and update the personalized smoking-reduction plan
  • To display progress and statistics inside the app
  • To send optional reminders

We do not:

  • Sell or share data with third parties for advertising
  • Use tracking across other apps or websites
  • Collect sensitive health data

Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Messaging

These services may collect non-personal device information under their own privacy policies.

Children

The App is intended for users 18+ due to smoking-related content. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

No Medical Advice

Quit Smoking 30 is not a medical product. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available through this page.
